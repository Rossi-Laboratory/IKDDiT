Introduction

IKDDiT is a research project that explores how diffusion models and knowledge distillation can be applied to improve overlay map generation in semiconductor photolithography. This page provides a quick overview of the motivation, approach, and results with visual figures taken from the main paper and supplementary materials.

Core Idea

Instead of training a large diffusion model from scratch, IKDDiT leverages a distilled teacher network that embeds semiconductor-specific knowledge. The student model then learns to generate overlay maps with both efficiency and fidelity, combining the strengths of diffusion sampling and knowledge transfer.

In short: a compact diffusion model enhanced by knowledge distillation for manufacturing data.
